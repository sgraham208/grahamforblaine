Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

Overview

Welcome to the website of Graham for Blaine ("We," "Us," "Our"), the campaign committee for Stephen Graham, candidate for Blaine County Clerk. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices for collection, use, and disclosure of your information when you use our website. By using this site, you agree that your use is governed by this Privacy Policy. We may update this policy from time to time and will indicate the date it was last updated. Your continued use of the site affirms your agreement to any changes.

Information We Collect

Voluntary Information: We may collect personal information that you voluntarily provide, including your name, email address, phone number, mailing address, and employer/occupation (as required by campaign finance law). You may provide this information when you sign up for email updates, volunteer, make a donation, or contact us.

Automatically Generated Information: We use Google Analytics (GA4) to understand how visitors use our website. This may collect non-personally identifiable information such as IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on pages. No personally identifiable information is collected through analytics. We do not associate analytics data with any personally identifiable information unless you explicitly submit that information via a form on our website.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to send campaign updates, event invitations, volunteer coordination messages, and fundraising communications. If you opt in to receive text messages, we will send campaign-related texts to the phone number you provide.

SMS/Text Messaging

If you opt in to receive text messages from Graham for Blaine, you consent to receive campaign updates, event notifications, volunteer coordination messages, and fundraising communications via SMS/MMS. Message frequency may vary. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help. Reply STOP to cancel.

No mobile information will be shared with third parties/affiliates for marketing/promotional purposes. Text messaging originator opt-in data and consent will not be shared with any third parties.

Except as noted in this section, we will not share SMS opt-in consent and data with third parties.

Information Sharing

We may share your information if: (1) you authorize us to do so; (2) it is necessary for our service providers (such as Mailchimp, ActBlue, or RumbleUp) to provide services on our behalf; (3) it is required by law, including campaign finance disclosure requirements; or (4) it is necessary to protect our rights or the safety of our users.

We may aggregate your information with that of others and share such information in a way that would not identify you individually.

Donations

Donation information is collected and processed by ActBlue. Federal and state campaign finance laws require disclosure of donor name, address, occupation, and employer for contributions over certain thresholds. This information becomes part of the public record as required by law. Any credit card information provided is only used to immediately process your donation.

Third-Party Services

We use Mailchimp for email, ActBlue for donations, Google Forms for volunteer signups, and RumbleUp for text messaging. These services have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of third-party sites linked from our website.

Cookies

Our site uses cookies from Google Analytics to collect anonymous traffic data. You can opt out of Google Analytics by visiting Google's opt-out page. If you wish to block cookies, refer to your browser's help documentation.

Security

We employ reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no data transmission over the Internet can be guaranteed as 100% secure.

Email

Submitting your email address on our site may result in your address being added to our email list. You may unsubscribe at any time using the "unsubscribe" link included in each email.

Opt Out

You may request to be removed from our email or text message lists at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any text message
  • Using the unsubscribe link in any email
  • Contacting us at info@grahamforblaine.com

Children

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at info@grahamforblaine.com or write to: Graham for Blaine, PO Box 395, Hailey, ID 83333.
