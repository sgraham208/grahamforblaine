About Stephen

Stephen Graham was first appointed Blaine County Clerk in September 2021 and subsequently won election. He holds a Master of Public Policy from Georgetown University and brings six years of experience managing multi-million-dollar global health programs in international development.

A classically trained violinist — Concertmaster of the Wood River Valley Orchestra and board member and violinist with The Chelsea Symphony in New York City — Stephen came to Idaho as an artist-in-residence at Ernest Hemingway's historic home in Ketchum. He lives in Hailey.

As Clerk, he serves as Clerk of the District Court, Ex-Officio Auditor, Chief Budget Officer, Ex-Officio Recorder, and Chief Elections Officer — five constitutional roles, one standard of excellence.

What We've Built

Modernized land records
Launched new recording software in 2025 after 18 months of planning. Free online access to document indexes, plus Fraud Notify alerts for property owners.
Enterprise financial system rollout
Leading Blaine County's transition to an enterprise-grade financial platform (2025–2027) — the most significant modernization of county financial operations in Blaine County history.
150+ student poll workers recruited
Since 2023, partnering with local schools to bring high school students into elections — building civic engagement from the ground up.
