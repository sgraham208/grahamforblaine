About Stephen

As Clerk, Stephen Graham serves as Clerk of the District Court, Ex-Officio Auditor, Chief Budget Officer, Ex-Officio Recorder, and Chief Elections Officer — five constitutional roles, one standard of excellence.

Stephen lives in the Woodside neighborhood in Hailey and serves as Concertmaster of the Wood River Valley Orchestra, a community orchestra. He came to Idaho in 2020 as an artist-in-residence at Ernest Hemingway's historic home in Ketchum — and stayed.

He was first appointed Blaine County Clerk in September 2021 and subsequently won election. He holds a Master of Public Policy from Georgetown University and brings over a decade of experience managing multi-million-dollar budgets — six years in international public health and five years as Blaine County's chief budget officer.

What We've Built

Highest voter turnout in Idaho.

Blaine County recorded the highest voter turnout in the state in the November 2025 election.

Voter information pamphlet.

Published candidate biographies and ballot text so residents could prepare before Election Day.

150+ student poll workers recruited.

Since 2023, partnering with local schools to bring high school students into elections — building civic engagement from the ground up.

Modernized land records.

Launched new recording software in 2025 after 18 months of planning. Free online access to document indexes, plus Fraud Notify alerts for property owners.

Enterprise financial system rollout.

Leading Blaine County's transition to an enterprise-grade financial platform — the most significant modernization of county financial operations in Blaine County history.

Budget transparency.

Built a multi-decade budget analytics database and published an annual budget book and financial report — so the public can see where every dollar goes.

Broadband to Picabo and Carey.

Extended broadband access to underserved communities through federal ARPA funding.

Expanded language access.

Sponsored advanced training for an Idaho Supreme Court–certified Spanish interpreter and additional staff pursuing interpreter certification.
